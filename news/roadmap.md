# 🗺️ ROADMAP — Universal Ontological Language

**Strategic Vision and Development Plan**

_Version 0.8.0 — April 2026_

---

## 🎯 Project Vision

**Mission:** Create a fully functional, systematic constructed language where ontological organization, compositional structure, and logical gradation combine into a practical and learnable communication system.

**Core Values:**
- **Systematic:** Every element follows predictable, derivable patterns
- **Compositional:** Meanings emerge transparently from component parts
- **Practical:** Designed for actual communication, not just theory
- **Expandable:** Open architecture for systematic growth
