### **Phonology Quick Reference**

**Consonants (20 base):**
```
P  /p/    T  /t/    K  /k/    B  /b/    M  /m/
G  /g/    V  /v/    R  /ɾ/    J̌ /dʒ/   S  /s/
F  /f/    H  /h/    N  /n/    D  /d/    L  /l/
Ľ  /ʎ/    W  /w/    X  /ʃ/    Y  /ʒ/    Z  /z/
```

**Consonant Clusters (10 total):**
```
Defined (7):  PR /pɾ/  BR /bɾ/  TR /tɾ/  DR /dɾ/  
              KR /kɾ/  GR /gɾ/  PL /pl/

Reserved (3): BL /bl/  KL /kl/  GL /gl/
```

**Vowels (5):**
```
a /a/    e /e/    i /i/    o /o/    u /u/
```

**Gradation Pattern:**
- **Standard (1-16):** a (minimum) → u (maximum)
- **Exception W (17):** a (positive) → u (negative)
- **Exception X,Y,Z (18-20):** a (maximum+) → u (minimum−)

## <a name="examples"></a>📝 Example Sentences

### **Basic Level Examples**

**Simple Statements:**
```
ba va = I do
be va = you do
bi va = he/she does
bo va = we do
bu va = you all/they do
```

**With Tense:**
```
ba va ta = I did (past)
ba va ti = I do (present)
ba va tu = I will do (future)
```

**With Location (P-family):**
```
ba va pi [location] = I work at [location]
be va po [location] = you come from [location]
bi va pa [location] = they go to [location]
```

### **Intermediate Level Examples**

**With Modifiers:**
```
[word]ma = small [word]
[word]mu = large [word]
[word]va = still [word]
[word]vu = moving [word]
```
