# 🧬 MATRIX P — Substance/Being

**Ontological Domain: What IS it?**

_Matter, entities, existence, composition_

---

## 📊 Overview

**Domain Letter:** P
**Domain Focus:** Substance, materiality, being, existence  
Contains: apa - epe - ipi - opo - upu
**Total Concepts:** 625 (5 subcategories × 25 vectors × 5 gradations)

**Covers:**

- What things are made of
- States of matter and existence
- Levels of complexity and organization
- Origins and sources
- Taxonomic classifications

---

## 🗂️ Subcategory Structure

|Code|Subcategory|Focus|
|---|---|---|
|**a-p**|Origin|Where does it come from? Natural, artificial, biological|
|**e-p**|Vitality|Is it alive? Dead, inert, living, sentient, conscious|
|**i-p**|Complexity|How organized? Atomic → cosmic scale|
|**o-p**|Function|What role does it serve? Structural, energy, information|
|**u-p**|Taxonomy|Pure ontological classification of substances|

---

## 📐 Gradation Key

Every vector has **5 gradation levels** indicated by the final vowel:

|Vowel|Meaning|Intensity|
|---|---|---|
|**-a**|Minimal, negative, absence|Almost none, barely|
|**-e**|Weak, low, beginning|Slight, emerging|
|**-i**|Neutral, standard, balanced|Normal, typical|
|**-o**|Strong, high, pronounced|Significant, clear|
|**-u**|Maximal, absolute, extreme|Complete, total|
